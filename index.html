About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Human-Computer Interaction Group led by Prof. Harald Reiterer at the University of Konstanz. I am part of the SFB-TRR 161 Project C01 (Quantifying Interaction) and was a visiting researcher at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA, USA, from October 2023 to April 2024. My research explores the potential and challenges of mixed reality-supported interactive exhibitions.
