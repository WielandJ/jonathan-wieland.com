About Me

I am a research assistant and PhD candidate in the Human-Computer Interaction Group led by Prof. Harald Reiterer at the University of Konstanz. I am part of the SFB-TRR 161 Project C01 (Quantifying Interaction) and was a visiting researcher in the Augmented Perception Lab at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA, USA, from October 2023 to April 2024. My research interests include exploring the potential and challenges of mixed reality-supported interactive exhibitions.
